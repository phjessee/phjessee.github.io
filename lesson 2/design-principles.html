White Space
Invision
InvisionApp.com

Invision App has a great example of white space. The moment you land on their page It's clean with very few a simple header that conveys their companies mission and 2 call-to-action buttons

Proximity
Microsoft
Microsoft.com

Microsoft uses the principle of proximity great. The buttons at the top are close together indicating they are all are similar. the text below that with the header "Come together" and the paragraph below that are close together which is indicating proximity or relation. This patter continues on with the image and the chat. the image shows a person chatting with the text below that which indicates relation/proximity

Alignment
Maze Design
MazeDesign.com

Maze Design is a designing platform and so I would expect them to follow design principles to a T. The alignment on the home screen is great. The navigation is aligned horizontally and the header, paragraph, button and images are all left aligned down the page. Doing that makes it look professionally done.

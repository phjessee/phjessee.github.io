City Information

Riverton is a city in Salt Lake County, Utah, United States. It is part of the Salt Lake City, Utah Metropolitan Statistical Area. The population was 38,753 at the 2010 census, but has since grown to 44,440. Riverton is located in the rapidly growing southwestern corner of the Salt Lake Valley.

Riverton City Businesses

Businesses thrive in Riverton, Utah! As the city and the surrounding area continues to grow in population, businesses continue to grow as well and new businesses come to the community. Riverton and directly adjacent cities of Bluffdale, Draper, Herriman and South Jordan have a combined population of over 220,000; making Riverton a prime place to locate a business.

At the heart of the southwest Salt Lake County region, Riverton is conveniently located along several key transportation corridors, giving businesses easy access customers and potential customers. Bangerter Highway, Mountain View Corridor and Redwood Road all cross directly through Riverton, and the city maintains easy access to I-15 via 12300/12600 S and Bangerter Highway.
